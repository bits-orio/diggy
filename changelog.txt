    - Initial scaffold: nauvis becomes a black void built into caves at runtime as you dig the frontier wall.

    - Light mixed starter ore patch inside the spawn carve-out.

    - Treasure chests sealed in the cover at seed-fixed locations; loot scales with depth.
    - Dig spawns: biters and rarer nests/worms appear only as dig consequences; expansion off by default.
    - Tree-cover patches: quick to mine, flammable, otherwise behave as rock cover.
    - Map starts dark except one chunk at spawn; the chart grows as you dig.
    - Sealed water pockets; per-surface dig tracking drives evolution (host-tunable).
    - Mid-save install detection with a clear warning (Diggy requires a new map).
